Introduction

Whether you're looking to improve your game or just have fun seeing your progress, the app offers detailed statistics and visualizations to make every throw count. To start your practice section, select Scoring from the menu.

Scoring Mode

Currently there is only one mode in the app, and that is the scoring mode. It allows you to store your throws, while showing some key statistics during your practice sessions.

There are three separate entry fields for each throw. That means, you enter the score of each dart, rather than the sum of them. When entering the score, the following apply:

  • Only valid score can be entered
  • Whitespaces are not allowed
  • Except for the special prefixes and entries, only numbers are allowed
  • Prefixes are case-insensitive

The following is a list of the special prefixes and entries:

"D" - double (usage: D16) "T" - triple (usage: T20) "B" - bouncer (usage: B) "25" - bull (usage: 25) "50" - bullseye (usage: 50) "0" - no score (usage: 0) "R" - robin hood (usage: R)

With the Submit button, you can submit your score. It will be then added to the throw history table next to the entry fields. If a non-valid score is entered, it will be shown in red and the score won't be submitted until all scores are valid.

Should the score be incorrectly submitted, you can double click on the corresponding row in the throw history table, and edit the incorrect entry.

The following statistics are shown during the practice sessions:

  • 3-dart-average
  • Nr. of darts thrown so far
  • Score so far
  • Current maximum score
  • Ratio of trebleless visits
